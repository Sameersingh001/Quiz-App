Rules of Game

  • 🧑‍💼 First, enter your name to start the quiz.
  • ✅ Select an answer from the given options for each question.
  • 👉 Click the “Next” button to go to the next question after selecting an option.
  • 🚫 Do not click “Submit” before completing all questions.
  • ⛔ Clicking “Submit” early may not show your complete score.
  • 📵 If options are not visible, then click the “Submit” button once to check your score.
  • 📊 Your final score will be shown after submission.
